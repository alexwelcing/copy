Case Study #002 - Legal Tech

Law.com: Intelligence Over Urgency

Repositioning a legal directory into an enterprise AI-powered intelligence platform.

The Challenge

Law.com needed to transform from a traditional legal directory into a premium AI-powered legal intelligence platform targeting Fortune 500 General Counsel. The campaign required a sophisticated enterprise positioning that breaks free from typical legal marketing clichés.

The Strategic Approach

We positioned Law.com as "Legal Intelligence Infrastructure" - mission-critical technology that empowers enterprise legal teams with AI-powered insights. The creative direction focused on speed, precision, and strategic advantage, using high-end tech aesthetics instead of traditional legal imagery.

Target Audience

Primary: General Counsel and Chief Legal Officers at enterprises ($500M+ revenue)

Secondary: Legal Operations Directors and Compliance Heads

Key Pain Point: Drowning in legal research, need faster strategic insights without sacrificing accuracy
