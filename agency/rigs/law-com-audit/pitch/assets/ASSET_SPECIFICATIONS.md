# Pitch Deck Visual Asset Specifications

## Design System

### Brand Colors
- **Primary Navy:** #0A1628
- **Gold Accent:** #C9A227
- **Cream Background:** #F8F6F1
- **Text White:** #FFFFFF
- **Alert Red:** #B8352E
- **Success Green:** #2E7D32

### Typography
- **Headlines:** DM Serif Display or similar
- **Body:** Inter or similar sans-serif
- **Data:** JetBrains Mono or similar monospace

### Style Direction
Premium consulting aesthetic. Think McKinsey meets Bloomberg. Clean, confident, data-forward. No clip art. No stock photos of handshakes. Sophisticated visualizations and abstract imagery that suggests intelligence and precision.

## Data Visualization Specifications

### Bar Charts
- Horizontal preferred for comparisons
- Golden/amber for Law.com
- Muted gray for competitors
- No gridlines, values on bars
- Dark backgrounds

### Line Charts
- Single accent color per line
- Subtle dots at data points
- No gridlines, minimal axis labels
- Dark backgrounds

### Pie/Donut Charts
- Donut style preferred
- Golden primary segment
- Complementary colors for others
- Center text for total/key metric

### Tables
- Alternating row shading (very subtle)
- Golden headers
- Right-aligned numbers
- No vertical lines

## Production Notes

1. **All assets should be generated at 2x resolution** for retina displays
2. **Export as PNG** with transparency where applicable
3. **Maintain consistent lighting** across all abstract visualizations
4. **Color grade for consistency** if generating multiple assets
5. **Test against dark mode** as deck may be viewed on various screens
